About Me

Driving Innovation with Enterprise-Grade Generative AI

I am a highly skilled AI Engineer with over 4 years of dedicated experience in developing and deploying production-grade AI solutions. My core passion lies in leveraging the power of Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems, and creating robust data pipelines to solve complex challenges and deliver tangible business value.

Core Expertise:

  • LLMs & RAG Systems: Designing, fine-tuning (LoRA), and deploying advanced language models for diverse applications.
  • Automated MLOps: Implementing CI/CD workflows (GitLab CI, Airflow) for resilient and scalable AI deployments.
  • Data Analytics & Pipelines: Building comprehensive data platforms and processing large-scale sensor data (AWS Glue, S3, BigQuery).
  • Computer Vision: Developing and deploying high-availability models for tasks like defect detection (TensorFlow, OpenCV).

My approach combines cutting-edge AI research with practical, results-oriented implementation. I thrive on transforming innovative ideas into scalable, enterprise-ready AI solutions that drive efficiency and growth. I'm always eager to explore new challenges and contribute my expertise to impactful projects in the realm of Generative AI.
